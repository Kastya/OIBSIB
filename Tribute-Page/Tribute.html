EIICHIRO ODA

"Surpass the best to be the best."



Eiichiro Oda, born January 1, 1975 in Kumamoto, is a Japanese manga artist, best known as the creator of the manga and anime One Piece.


Here is some short information about this man.

    Appearance In real life, Oda is a Japanese man of average height, with few (if any) distinguishing features. When not in formal settings, he favors casual, Western-style clothing.Like most mangaka, Oda typically uses outlandish caricatures to represent himself in printed works. The most common of these is a striped tropical fish, best known for "giving" all of Oda's weekly comments in Shonen Jump's table of contents; in more detailed depictions (such as the Color Walks' Monochrome Talk headers), this fish is usually given human ears and affixed atop a human body like a mask.



Eiichiro Oda has received several awards and titles.
